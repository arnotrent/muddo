Who We Are

MACL — Muddo Agro Chemicals LTD

Based in Kampala's Container Village Nakivubo, Equity Bank Basement V013, Muddo Agro Chemicals LTD (MACL) is a Ministry of Agriculture (MAAIF)-registered distributor of pesticides, herbicides, fungicides, fertilizers and spraying equipment serving farmers across all regions of Uganda.

We partner directly with manufacturers in India, China and Europe to bring authentic, proven agrochemical solutions at competitive prices — with zero counterfeit products, ever.
